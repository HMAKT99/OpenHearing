# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

OpenHearing is designed to collect as little as possible — effectively nothing.

## What OpenHearing does with your data

- **Microphone:** In assist mode, OpenHearing captures audio from the microphone,
  processes it in real time, and plays it back to your headset. **This audio is
  never recorded, stored, or transmitted.** It exists only momentarily in memory
  while being processed and is then discarded.
- **Hearing screening results & profile:** Your audiogram and settings are stored
  **only on your device** (local app storage). They are never uploaded.
- **No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no trackers, no network calls.** The app
  does not include any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not send
  your data anywhere.

## Permissions

- **RECORD_AUDIO** — required for assist mode (live amplification). Used only while
  assist mode is on.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE** — to keep assist mode
  running with a visible, ongoing notification while you use other apps.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** — to show that notification (Android 13+).

## Data sharing

None. Your data never leaves your device through OpenHearing.

## Children

OpenHearing is a general-audience hearing-assistance tool and is not directed at
children.
