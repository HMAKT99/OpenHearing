OpenHearing is a free, open-source hearing-assistance app for Android. It runs a
pure-tone hearing screening, builds a personalized amplification profile from the
result, and uses it to amplify quiet sound — like speech — in real time, with any
headset or earbuds.

Features:
* Pure-tone hearing screening (per ear, per frequency) with an adaptive staircase
* A personalized amplification profile derived from your screening
* Real-time hearing assist: multi-band gain, dynamic-range compression, and a
  feedback/howl guard
* A hard output-loudness limiter and a comfort calibration so sound never gets
  uncomfortably loud, plus an always-available instant stop
* Accessibility-first: large controls, high-contrast theme, scalable text
* Completely private: no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no network access. Audio
  is processed on-device and never recorded or transmitted.

IMPORTANT: OpenHearing is a sound-amplification and hearing-assistance tool. It is
NOT a medical device, NOT a certified hearing aid, and NOT a substitute for a
professional hearing exam. It does not diagnose or treat any condition. If you
have concerns about your hearing, please see an audiologist or doctor. Keep the
volume comfortable and stop if anything is too loud.
